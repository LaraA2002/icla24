archive
1 of 2
noun 



ar·​chive

ˈär-ˌkīv 









Synonyms of archive



1 





: a place in which public records or historical materials (such as documents) are preserved
an archive of historical manuscripts a film archive


also
: the material preserved 

—often used in plural 
reading through the archives 
 
 

 

2 





: a repository or collection especially of information


 

 









archive
2 of 2
verb 







archived; archiving 





transitive verb






: to file or collect in or as if in an archive
archive documents archived tissue samples

library
